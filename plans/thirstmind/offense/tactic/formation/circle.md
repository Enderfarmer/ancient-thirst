# Cirle formation

## Structure (lower number means closer to target)

1. Psychedelic and strong melee units (Psychedelics behind the player)

2. Weak ranged units
3. Weak block units

## Unit numbers

- 2 Psychedelic units
- 12 Melee units
- 5 Ranged units
- 5 Block units

## Forming

2 Melee units distract the player while the others form in a circle, rangers hide behind them while the block units use the given time for infecting the ground
