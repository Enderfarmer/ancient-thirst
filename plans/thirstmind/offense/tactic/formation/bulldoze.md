# Bulldoze formation

## Structure (lower numbers mean closer to the target)

1. Max thirstforce shield unit
2. Melee units
3. Min block units
4. Long-ranged units

## Unit numbers

- 1 Shield unit
- 10 Melee units
- 5 Block units
- 3 Ranged units

## Forming

10 Melee units form a 5x2 wall, block units hide behind each back unit, rangers support them from the back and lastly, a shield unit is brought by aviation.
